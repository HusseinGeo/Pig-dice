PIG DICE

RULES:

  1. To play you will need 2 to 10 players, one 6-sided dice, and a pencil and some paper for keeping score.
  2. If a player rolls a 1, they score nothing and loses all points he/she accumulated that turn, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  3. If a player chooses to "hold", their turntotal is added to their score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  4. Play passes from player to player until a winner is determined.
  5. First player to score 100 or more points wins.
